About Us

Kadhiyezhili is a non-governmental organization dedicated to uplifting underprivileged communities through education, awareness, and empowerment. We believe that small acts of kindness can spark significant change. Our goal is to build a world where every individual has equal access to opportunity, dignity, and hope.

What We Do

  • Education Support – Providing school supplies, scholarships, and tuition for underprivileged children.
  • Environmental Awareness – Tree plantations, plastic-free drives, and eco-awareness camps.
  • Women Empowerment – Skill training, self-help groups, and leadership programs.
  • Health & Hygiene – Organizing medical camps and hygiene awareness in rural areas
